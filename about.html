About Us

Taras Engineering LLC a Construction Project Management Company, working with investors to manage a project. Our company oversees all aspects of construction process, working closely with engineers and architects to develop a plan, establish a timetable, and determine labor and material cost. The Project Manager is responsible for ensuring the project is completed on budget and within scope. We also hire and manage subcontractors, gather permits, and provide all details of the project are up to code. The primary construction project manager's responsibilities are overall project planning, distributing resources, time management, risk management, setting benchmarks, managing the budget, managing staff, and developing relationships with key stakeholders
