Interim Management

Whether you own a business or project that has been inactive for some time, or an industry-leading, profitable business, you may be dissatisfied with your current state of affairs. If you are seeking change, have the will to go through the changes, and are looking for a reliable partner, we are ready to join your team. Upon your request we will provide a more detailed example of our interim management work aside from those described on our main page. In the next slide we’ll explain our general approach to interim management.

Our approach to interim management of clients’ businesses

  • ✓ Determine where we are (or the start-off point of the Client's business).
  • ✓ Determine strategic resources upon which we can build a new vision for the business.
  • ✓ Identify the most attractive internal and external market opportunities.
  • ✓ Combine the results of the first three steps into a detailed vision of the company's development, get it approved by company’s key stakeholders, prioritize and assign the implementation of the strategy changes within a specified timeline.
  • ✓ Help communicate the changes and new vision within the company.
  • ✓ Update existing or develop a new organizational structure (sometimes also a new legal structure), corresponding to the new vision, strengthen company’s team with the right people (we can oversee the recruitment or provide support in selection process).
  • ✓ Update or develop new motivation, evaluation, accounting, reporting, and control systems.
  • ✓ Develop parts of the strategy plan in details (regardless of the business aspect – marketing, logistics, IT, etc.) and help coordinate the implementation of the most complicated parts of the project. We regularly develop new products, new brands (incl. positioning and naming), their pricing, the approach to their distribution, and all aspects of promotion, including digital promotion.
  • ✓ Depending on a particular situation, a different number of the above steps can be carried out simultaneously. Also, the sequence of the steps may somewhat differ from case to case. For example, the company situation is sometimes such that once it’s clear that we need a new, much more vital sales force as soon as possible, we start building it fast. At the same time, we continue developing the overall detailed vision of what the company will focus on over the next several years.

Self-Sustainable Business



During the whole period of change, we usually don’t take formal positions. Yet, through the close cooperation and approval of our steps with the Owner (“all” or only “key” steps, depending on the Owner’s choice of level of detail they want to be involved in), we exert enough influence to drive change of any depth and scale. We aim to help create beautiful, commercially aggressive, highly profitable, and - what’s also very important - self-sustainable businesses. By the latter, we mean that the whole business system must function properly after leaving the company.
